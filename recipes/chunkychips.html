Chunky Chips

Description

This recipe is for amazing chunky oven chips

Ingredients

  • 500g Maris Pipers
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Olive Oil
  1. Pre-heat oven to 230Â°c
  2. Cut potatoes into chunky chip sizes with the skin on and par boil in salted water for 5 minutes
  3. Remove from the boil and drain
  4. Evenly spread the potatoes out on to a baking tray, use multiple if needed
  5. Coat in seasoning and oil, toss them to ensure even coverage
  6. Cook in the over for 30 minutes and flip them for the last 15-20 minutes
  7. Dish them up and enjoy!
